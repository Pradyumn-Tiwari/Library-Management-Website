Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1How do I borrow books from the library management website?

To borrow books from our library management website, simply log in to your account using your username and password. Then, browse the library's collection and select the book(s) you wish to borrow. Click on the "Borrow" button next to the book's listing, and it will be added to your account. You can then check out your selected books for the specified borrowing period.

2 Can I renew my borrowed books online?

Yes, you can renew your borrowed books online through our library management website. Log in to your account and navigate to your borrowed items.

3How can I search for specific books or materials in the library's collection?

You can easily search for specific books or materials in our library's collection by using the search bar located on our website's homepage.

4 What are the procedures for returning books borrowed online?

Returning books borrowed online is simple and convenient. Once you've finished reading a book, log in to your account on our library management website. Navigate to your borrowed items and select the book(s) you wish to return. Follow the prompts to initiate the return process. Depending on the library's policies, you may have the option to return the books physically to the library or through designated drop-off points.

5 Is there a limit to the number of books I can borrow at one time through the website?

Yes, there may be a limit to the number of books you can borrow at one time through our website, which is typically determined by your membership type and the library's borrowing policies.
